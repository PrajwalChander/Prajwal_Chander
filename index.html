Hire Neighbourhood



"Hire Neighbourhood" is an innovative online platform that connects individuals and businesses with local workers in their community.Our project aims to streamline the process of finding and hiring skilled professionals for various tasks, fostering a sense of community collaboration and empowering local economies.
With Hire Neighbourhood, users can easily browse through a diverse range of local workers who offer their services in areas such as home repairs, gardening, cleaning, tutoring, event planning, and much more. Whether you need assistance with a small task or a larger project, our platform simplifies the process of finding the right professional for the job.

In developing Hire Neighbourhood, I utilized a diverse range of skills to create an effective and user-friendly platform. Here are the key skills that were employed:

  • Front-End Development: I leveraged my proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create an intuitive and visually appealing user interface. By implementing responsive design principles, I ensured that the platform is accessible and functional across various devices and screen sizes.
  • User Experience (UX) Design: I incorporated UX design principles to create a smooth and enjoyable experience for users. Through careful consideration of user flows, information architecture, and visual design, I aimed to optimize usability and engagement on the platform.
  • Database Management: I designed and implemented a robust database structure using modern database management systems. This allowed for retrieval of relevant data.
